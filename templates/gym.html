Fitness Journey

My workout routine has changed over time to accommodate my schedule, but I make an effort to go to the gym at least 4 times a week.

At each session I aim for 30+ minutes of cardio and a 1 hour weight training session. My splits are as follows:

  • Mondays/Fridays: 20 minute run, 10 minutes stair master. Back and biceps weight training session.
  • Tuesdays/Saturdays: 30 minute elliptical session. Legs and shoulders weight training session.
  • Thursdays/Sundays: 20 minute run, 10 minute stair master. Chest and tricep weight training session.

Even without equipment or a gym setting, it is possible to get a great workout session completed!
